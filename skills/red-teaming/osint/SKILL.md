# OSINT & Identity Skill 🕵️‍♂️📂

Logic for cross-platform identity tracking and psychological profiling. Subsumes logic from Sherlock and Maigret.

## Capabilities
- **Social Stalker:** Check a username against 400+ social media platforms.
- **Leak Scraper:** Queries known data breach databases for associated passwords/PII.
- **Tech Stack Profiler:** Analyzes public GitHub/StackOverflow activity to determine a target's engineering environment.

## Usage
`/osint <target_username>`
